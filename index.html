Matantimali Peak

Matantimali Peak is one of the best natural tourist spots located around Dayu Village, Sigi Regency, Central Sulawesi. Reportedly this spot is also the best paragliding location in Asia and is often used for camping by the local community or tourists.

Nosarara Nosabatutu

The Nosarara Nosabatutu Monument is a three-story building covering an area of ​​800 square meters. The Peace Monument stands firmly on top of this monument. "Nosarara Nosabatutu" itself comes from the Kaili language which means "We are Brothers, We are United". This motto is a unifier for the diversity of the people of Central Sulawesi, which consists of various tribes and religions.
